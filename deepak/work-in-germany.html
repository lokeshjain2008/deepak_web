Work in Germany

Mobilitas Training Private Limited, India, is offering a CERTIFIED TRAINING & INTERGRATION Program for experienced B.Sc. Nursing Professionals in cooperation with Horizont Training UG, a healthcare service provider based in Federal City of Bonn, Germany (www.horizont-training.com).

With a focus on integrating high quality nursing skills from India to Germany, our international team is committed to train and assist licensed & experienced nursing professionals from different locations in India (Kochi, Kottayam, Chennai, Delhi & Mumbai).

Who you are

  • A Bachelor Degree in Nursing (B.Sc. as per Indian Nursing Council)
  • Minimum 2 years of work experience
  • Sufficient computer skills (MS Office, Email etc.)
  • Eligible to apply for a German Visa
  • Mandatory participation in our Certified Training and Intergration Program.
  • Willigness to commit to the Future Employer for a minimum period of time
  • Ready to take up the challenge and financial expenses

How we proceed

  • Intial Screening & Registration
  • Verification Process & Candidate Profiling
  • Evaluation of German Language Skills
  • Integration Training & Preparatory Sessions
  • Horizont Training UG Certification
  • Job Interviews with the employer
  • Recognition and Licensing Process
  • Visa Processing & Work Permit
  • Deployment & Integration

    Our offer for you in detail.

    • A clear Orientation about our Program
    • Support and guidance to reach the mandatory Language Requirements
    • Candidate Profiling & Preparatory Sessions
    • Training for successful Integration Process (Adaptation Course; Application for Equivalence Test etc.)
    • Horizont Training UG Certification (after passing the exam, each candidate receives a certificate)
    • Training for Job Interview
    • Mediation to higly reputed Employers and best Salary Packages in the market
    • Recognition Procedure (Qualifications & Work Experience)
    • Assistance in Visa Processing & Work Permit
    • Coordination of Pre-departure & Arrival in Germany
    • Assistance for finding accomodation in Germany
    • Assistance for finding best Health Insurance-, Banking-, and Telephone Partners
    • Further personal assistance up to 6 months from the arrival date in Germany (including Emigrant Registration Rules, added courses as per German Healthcare Standards)

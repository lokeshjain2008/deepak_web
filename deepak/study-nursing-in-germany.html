Study in Germany

Mobilitas Training Private Limited, India, is offering an INTERGRATION Program for pursuing a three year nursing degree in Germany in cooperation with Horizont Training UG, a healthcare service provider based in Federal City of Bonn, Germany (www.horizont-training.com). With a focus on developing skilled nursing professionals in Germany, our international team is committed to assist nursing aspirants from different locations in India.

About the Nursing Program

  • 3 year nursing degree program as per German Healthcare System (6 Semesters)
  • Course Structure as per national standards
  • Nursing program in orientation with old age care
  • No semester fee
  • Langauge of instruction is German
  • Scholarships and monthly financial support for each individual untill the course completion
  • Full time work permit with all national social benefits upon course completion
  • Semester holidays as per national rules & regulations
  • Complete guidance for 36 months by Horizont Training UG, Germany team.
  • Course starting on April 2018

A perfect candidate for us who can apply for the program

  • Mandatory higher secondary education (+2) or an equivalent education
  • Minimum age of 18 years or above
  • Sufficient computer skills (MS Office, Email etc.)
  • Eligible to apply for a German Visa
  • Willingness to acquire mandatory B2 German Language skills
  • Mandatory participation in Horizont Training UG Certified Intergration Program
  • Willigness to commit to the course duration of 36 months with the Future Nursing School or Institution
  • Ready to take up the challenge and meet primary financial expenses

    Our offer for you in detail
